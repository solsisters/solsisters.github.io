Solution to Design Brief

The 2019 design brief asks the students to code a website for a band or musician of the students’ choice. Students were supposed to demonstrate their knowledge of coding by including components such as lyrics, tour dates, and a blog. Our team decided to use this design brief as a positive outlet to express creativity. We created a band called the Sol Sisters, and they are a group of four young, hardworking, and independent women that formed a band to help spread positivity and confidence through music. The website provides fans a way to look at tickets, lyrics, tour dates, and general information. Not only that, but the website gives an opportunity for admirers to buy the Sol Sister’s unique merch or participate in the fan-based blog. Additionally, we were able to add our own personal styles to our project while still following the design brief. For example, our band’s logo and name embodies a sun which correlates to happiness and positivity. We also chose to incorporate young women into the band to show our support for women in the technological field, since women’s rights are a growing topic in today's society.
